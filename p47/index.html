The Blueprint

Why the Belly?

1. Genetics: South Asian background means lower insulin sensitivity. Your body prefers storing carbs as belly fat rather than muscle fuel.

2. Skinny Fat: You aren't overweight, you are "under-muscled." Dieting makes you look frail. Heavy lifting is the only cure.

The Fix

  • Heavy Compounds: Squats/Deadlifts release testosterone. This attacks the belly fat hormonally.
  • The 10m Rule: Walking immediately after dinner forces glucose into muscles, preventing it from being stored as fat while you sleep.
  • Stress (Cortisol): High stress moves fat to the belly. Sleep & walking lower stress.

Timing is Everything

Eat Carbs Post-Workout & Dinner
Avoid Carbs Breakfast (Eat Eggs/Nuts)
